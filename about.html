About Us

Celebrating the most amiable and relaxed animals on Earth

Who We Are

We are a global group of capybara lovers that value their friendliness, calmness, social skills, and cuteness. We discuss everything related to capybaras, including entertaining activities, fan art, and business. Our community hosts capybara enthusiasts for feeding, care, and sharing stories, advice, and images. We welcome new members, whether experienced or distant, to appreciate these adorable animals' unique attributes.

Our Mission

To disseminate positive energy, joy, and awareness regarding the capabilities and stories of the capybara.

Our Vision

To collaboratively establish a welcoming and relaxed community that is both equitable and regenerative, fostering connections among capybara enthusiasts worldwide.
